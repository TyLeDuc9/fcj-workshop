## Areas for Improvement

Although I gained valuable knowledge and skills throughout my internship, I recognize that there are still several areas where I can continue to improve:

* Better comply with the company's rules, regulations, and working procedures.
* Strengthen my problem-solving skills and become more confident in expressing ideas and opinions during team discussions.
* Improve my time management and task prioritization skills to enhance overall work efficiency.
* Continue learning and keeping up to date with new technologies, especially AWS cloud services and solutions, to better meet future professional requirements.
